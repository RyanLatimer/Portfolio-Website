Benefits of Open Source

Open Source brings to the table many benefits particularly in the software scene. It provides a way to decentralize products, ideas, and services from a corporation whose first objective is to make money. When the creators of something are less concerned about making money and more concerned about making something great, the product becomes much better and can achieve much more. Open source software sees massive benefits from the fact that anyone can contribute to the project. Development of major milestones occurs many times fast than with normal proprietary software. This is because the community can come together to solve problems and create new features. Bug fixing and identification of niche ideas are far faster and much more affected as many of the developers are users and want to build the best possible product for themselves to use and for anyone else to use. Open source software is a way to develop more relevant software faster and inclusively while keeping it free and available for everyone.
