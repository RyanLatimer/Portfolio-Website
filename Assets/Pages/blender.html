Getting Started with Blender

I began working with blender when I was ten years old. I made it through the inaugural donut tutorial, but as a result of my age did not continue with the software. I recently got back into the software and have relearned the basics and am continuing to learn more advanced techniques and make more complex renders.

First Projects

After I completed the donut tutorial (for the second time) I worked on building a scene to represent a logo. I took an artistic and painted logo, and built a 3D photorealistic scene to represent it. The camera would then pull back, revealing that the scene was a part of the logo.

Going Forward

As I progressed, I moved into more complex projects and renders including space scenes, building assets for games, and future projects. I have also begun more complex renders that involve simulated physics of rigid bodies and water. I have really enjoyed the creative aspect of Blender and will enjoy continuing to work with it.
